Did You Know?

The hedgehog got its name because of its peculiar foraging habits. They root through hedges and other undergrowth in search of their favourite food - small creatures such as insects, worms, centipedes, snails, mice, frogs, and snakes. As it moves through the hedges it emits pig-like grunts â€” thus, the name hedgehog.

Did You Know?

The hedgehog is nocturnal, coming out at night and spending the day sleeping in a nest under bushes or thick shrubs.

Did You Know?

While hunting for food, they rely primarily upon their senses of hearing and smell because their eyesight is weak though their eyes are adapted for night-time vision.
